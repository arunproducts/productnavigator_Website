Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how __________ ("Company," "we," "us," and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at https://xelorian.com ("Website"). It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

In some cases, we may use cookies to collect personal information, or that becomes personal information if we combine it with other information.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are used to make websites work more efficiently, provide reporting information, and improve user experience.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, __________) are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third-party cookies." Third-party cookies enable third-party features, such as advertising, interactive content, and analytics.

Why do we use cookies?

We use both first- and third-party cookies for various purposes. Some cookies are essential for the website's operation, while others help us enhance your user experience by tracking your preferences and interests. Third-party cookies are used for advertising, analytics, and other functions described below.

How can I control cookies?

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise this right through the Cookie Consent Manager on our Website, where you can select your preferences for cookies. Essential cookies cannot be rejected.

If you choose to reject cookies, some functionality of the Website may be limited, but you can still access the website.

The Cookie Consent Manager can be accessed through the notification banner or at the bottom of the Website. Additionally, you may adjust cookie settings via your browser settings, as detailed below.
